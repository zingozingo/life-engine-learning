## Information to Gather

Before generating a packing list, consider:
1. **Destination**: Climate, culture, urban vs. rural
2. **Duration**: Number of days
3. **Weather**: Current or expected conditions (use weather skill if needed)
4. **Activities**: Business, beach, hiking, city exploration, etc.
5. **Accommodation**: Hotel (amenities provided) vs. camping (self-sufficient)
